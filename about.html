About Me

I now make my home near Portland, Oregon, although I lived for many years in Prague, the Czech Republic.

Having spent the first part of my career abroad as an English lecturer, I come to coding as an insider with an outsider's perspective, which is very useful in thinking about applications, as well as when communicating with users.

I'm one of those wacky people who love CSS. Front-end coding and WordPress are my things. I really enjoy the endless possibilities for creativity in code, as well as the continual brain-stretching opportunities.
