Ageless Beauty & Wellness

Custom WordPress Website from Company Blocks

Problem:

The designer wanted to display featured items and photos from the client's marketing blog on the fron page, and in addition, the night before the site was to go live, the client said she really needed video links on the front page as well.

Approach:

Using PHP and CSS animations, I designed a block which displayed 3 featured images and an excerpt from the blog posts when hovered. Because the site was to go live the next day, I redesigned the marketing block with CSS animations to indicate that the links led to videos, and the client was delighted.
